Automata Tutor Privacy Statement

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy statement explains many of the data collection and use practices of Automata Tutor.

Collection and Use of Your Information

Some information we collect may be used for further research, in collaboration with other researchers. Specifically, the information we collect from you can be used for

  1. to analyze and improve Automata Tutor,
  2. for further research on automata theory and education,
  3. to publish statistical information and results.
We will not publish any data about individual users.

In order to access certain Automata Tutor online services, you will be asked to enter your name, e-mail address, and a password. We will not share your email and password with any third parties, nor will we publish this information in any way.

Changes to this privacy statement

We will occasionally update this privacy statement to reflect changes in Automata Tutor. When we post changes, we will revise the "last updated" date at the bottom of this statement. We encourage you to periodically review this statement.
